Common Grounds. Local Roots.

Common Grounds Cafe and Co is a social enterprise of Nehemiah Project 38008 that serves as THE spot for people to relax, socialize, dine, work and support the community. At Common Grounds, we serve coffee beverages that start with high-quality coffee beans roasted right here in West Tennessee, a delicious selection of other beverages, and plenty of freshly prepared food to satisfy your appetite. We also offer free Wi-Fi and meeting space. It is our pleasure to offer a warm and positive atmosphere where all are welcome, and we thank you for your support in our mission!

To learn more about how Common Grounds Café and Co is helping NP3 make strides toward workforce development in Hardeman County, visit NP38008.org
